# Risks, Abuse Cases, and Controls

## Fraud and Abuse Vectors
- Click fraud and bot traffic.
- Self-referrals or merchant-owned affiliates.
- Fake conversions or synthetic orders.
- Refunds, chargebacks, and post-payout disputes.
- Attribution hijacking and cookie stuffing.

## Controls and Mitigations
- Bot detection, rate limits, and IP/device fingerprinting.
- Conversion verification via signed server-side webhooks.
- Payout hold periods with refund/chargeback signals.
- Velocity checks and anomaly detection on conversion rates.
- Affiliate verification tiers and risk scoring.

## Compliance Considerations
- KYC/AML for crypto payouts and high-risk affiliates.
- Data privacy: GDPR/CCPA consent and data retention policies.
- Ad platform policy compliance and prohibited categories.

## Operational Procedures
- Manual review queues for flagged campaigns.
- Payout freeze workflows with audit logs.
- Dispute resolution and affiliate appeal process.
