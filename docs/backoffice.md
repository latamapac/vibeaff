# Backoffice and Admin Features

## Dashboard
- Program KPIs: clicks, conversions, CVR, CAC, ROAS.
- Payout queue with hold status and risk flags.
- Affiliate performance and cohort reporting.

## Integrations Management
- Analytics connections (GA and alternates).
- Ads library ingestion and refresh status.
- Payment/crypto rails setup and verification.

## Risk and Compliance
- Fraud alerts with investigation workflow.
- KYC/AML status for affiliates.
- Audit logs for payouts and program changes.

## Operations
- Manual payout approval and batch releases.
- Campaign pause and affiliate suspension.
- Dispute resolution and notes.
